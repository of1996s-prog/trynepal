Put your ad images/gifs in this folder (or upload them through the
/admin Decap CMS media picker, which saves them here automatically).

Avoid spaces in filenames — use dashes instead — since spaces in a URL
need extra encoding and can break links. For example:

  postadv1st-nike.jpg        (post ad #1 — shown at the start of every post)
  postadv2nd-nike.jpg        (post ad #2 — shown after the first third)
  postadv3rd-adidas.gif      (post ad #3 — shown after the second third)
  postadv4th-adidas.jpg      (post ad #4 — shown at the end of every post)

  mainadv1.jpg               (sidebar ad shown while a visitor starts scrolling)
  mainadv2.jpg               (next sidebar ad, shown further down the page)
  mainadv3.jpg               (add as many mainadvN files as you like)

After uploading, update the filenames in content/ads.json (or through
/admin) to match exactly what you uploaded here.
